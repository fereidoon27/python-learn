# MacBook Text Navigation Shortcuts

## Word Navigation
- **Move to next word**: `Option + Right Arrow`
- **Move to previous word**: `Option + Left Arrow`

## Line Navigation
- **Move to start of line**: `Command + Left Arrow`
- **Move to end of line**: `Command + Right Arrow`

## Delete
- **Delete to start of line**: `Command + Delete`
- **Delete previous word**: `Option + Delete`
- **forward delete**: `fn` + Delete`

## Text Selection
- **Select next word**: `Shift + Option + Right Arrow`
- **Select previous word**: `Shift + Option + Left Arrow`
- **Select entire line**: `Command + Shift + Right/Left Arrow`

## Document Navigation
- **Move to top of document**: `Command + Up Arrow`
- **Move to bottom of document**: `Command + Down Arrow`
- **Page Up**: `Fn + Up Arrow`
- **Page Down**: `Fn + Down Arrow`


## Editing Shortcuts
- **Undo**: `Command + Z`
- **Redo**: `Command + Shift + Z`
